RESSONOTES MUSIC

Making Jesus Famous!

We love good, gospel music, and we create them in every opportunity we get. We also like to bless people with the healing power and annointing available in Jesus' name through inspiring music. We do not just sing, sing, and sing tho, we get around and we play, we love and we have fun. You just want to be a part of us, I promise! we also minister at events, and would surely love to bless as many people with our inspired lyrics and energetic rendition. We are more than a band, we are one big family.
